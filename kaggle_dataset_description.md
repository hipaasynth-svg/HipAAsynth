# Kaggle Dataset Description

This dataset consists of synthetic healthcare data generated for research and educational purposes. It aims to provide a realistic yet anonymized representation of patient information, medical history, clinical observations, and treatment plans.

## Dataset Features:
- **Patient ID**: Unique identifier for each patient.
- **Age**: Age of the patient.
- **Gender**: Gender of the patient (Male/Female/Other).
- **Diagnosis**: Clinical diagnosis information.
- **Symptoms**: Recorded symptoms experienced by the patient.
- **Treatment**: Details of the treatment provided.
- **Outcome**: Final health outcome of the patient after treatment.

## Purpose:
This dataset is intended for use in machine learning and statistical analysis, helping researchers and developers to:
- Test algorithms in a controlled environment.
- Develop predictive models for healthcare outcomes.
- Understand trends and patterns in synthetic clinical data without compromising real patient privacy.
